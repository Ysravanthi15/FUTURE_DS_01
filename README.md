# FUTURE_DS_01
**Dashboard Overview**

The E-Commerce Sales Report Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance across multiple dimensions such as revenue, customers, orders, time, and geography. It is designed to give stakeholders a clear and interactive view of business performance, enabling quick decision-making through visually rich insights. The dashboard integrates KPIs, trend analysis, and geographic distribution for a holistic understanding of sales data.

**Task Description**

The objective of this task was to develop an interactive Power BI dashboard that:
-Tracks key performance indicators like Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, and Customer Count
-Analyzes revenue trends over time (monthly basis)
-Identifies top-performing product descriptions
-Examines customer-wise revenue contributions
-Provides geographic insights using a map visualization
-Enables filtering by Country and Year for dynamic analysis

**Key Insights & Learnings**
-Achieved a strong understanding of data modeling and visualization techniques in Power BI
-Learned to create interactive dashboards using slicers (Country & Year filters)
-Utilized KPIs effectively to highlight business performance at a glance
-Improved skills in identifying patterns, trends, and outliers in large datasets
-Gained experience in presenting data in a clear and business-friendly format

**Insights from the Dashboard**
-Total Revenue (974.8K) indicates strong overall sales performance
-26K total orders and 376.36 average order value show consistent purchasing behavior
-Revenue trend shows a declining pattern over months, suggesting possible seasonal or demand fluctuations
-A few product descriptions contribute significantly (up to 206K revenue), indicating high-performing products
-Customer-level analysis reveals uneven revenue distribution, where a small group of customers generates higher revenue
-Geographic map highlights major sales concentration in Europe, with additional activity in other regions
-Filters allow detailed exploration, helping identify region-specific and time-based trends

**Conclusion**
The dashboard effectively transforms raw e-commerce data into actionable insights by combining KPIs, trend analysis, and geographic visualization. It enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed business decisions. This project demonstrates strong capabilities in data visualization, business intelligence, and analytical thinking.
